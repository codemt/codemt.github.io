ABOUT US
Rami Chemicals is India's leading chemical company work for various industrial requirement.

We call ourselves one stop solution when it comes to supply Speciality Chemicals and Services; we treat ETP and Cooling tower, Boiler Treatment Chemicals, Defoamers, RO Chemicals, Enzymes, Activated Carbon, CIP Chemicals etc. We strongly believe in after sales services.

We also supply Food Grade Chemicals for F&B industries, Paper Industry, Sugar Industry our product are Halal and Kosher certified.

We understand every client is important and different, hence we study every client and provide customized chemicals as per requirement and our technical team is always present for assistance.

Throughout our evolution from Trading Chemical Company to diversified Excipient & Ingredients conglomerate, to a global speciality chemicals company, we’ve advanced by becoming ever more capable of adding unique value to customer products.

We have thus attained huge clientele across the world owing to our customer-centric approach, transparent transaction policies and ethical business practices. We are committed to provide world class quality at most reasonable cost to stay competitive in the world market. Our aim is to support you reducing chemicals consumption, focusing on environmental respect and sustainability.
